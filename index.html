About

Andy Watson: Living for Beauty as best I can.

Of all the spiritual Goods, Beauty is the least dangerous. Except for Love.

With Goodness and Truth, people are sorely tempted to live for heroics and certainty. Justice is famously the worst temptor. How much Evil has been unleashed in the name of "Justice"?!?

But Beauty comes with no grand ambitions beyond staying within the Bounds and laughing with the infinite worlds.

Love? Well, of course I try to live also in and through and for Pure Love. But that's another story.

Actually, it's lots of stories. Pure Love & User's Manual or A Readable Reader might be good introductions. Or Fixing Frankenstein loops into familiar tales.
